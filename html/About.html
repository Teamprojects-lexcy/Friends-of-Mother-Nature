About Us

Empowering & Transforming Communities

We are a non-profit organization that believes in the fundamental right of every human being to access basic necessities such as clean water. We are committed to making a positive impact on the lives of communities, particularly women in Tharaka Nithi County, who face immense challenges due to the lack of access to clean water.

Our founder, Sarah, was moved by the struggles faced by women in Tharaka Nithi County who had to walk for hours every day to collect water from distant sources. Sarah recognized the potential of providing clean water to these communities to bring about significant change. Inspired by this vision, our organization was founded with the goal of empowering women by providing them with a reliable source of clean water that can be used for daily needs such as drinking, cooking, and bathing, as well as for irrigation to support agriculture.

Our primary focus is on drilling boreholes in the community, providing a sustainable and reliable source of clean water. We work closely with the local community to ensure that our efforts are sustainable and have a long-lasting impact on the lives of the women we aim to empower. Our approach is inclusive and participatory, involving community members in decision-making and implementation, to ensure that our interventions are culturally appropriate and meet the specific needs of the communities we serve.

We strongly believe that access to clean water is a key enabler for social and economic development. By providing access to clean water, we are not only improving the health and well-being of the women and their families but also creating opportunities for them to pursue education and income-generating activities. Our efforts are focused on empowering women to become agents of change in their communities, ensuring that the benefits of our interventions are sustained and multiplied.
